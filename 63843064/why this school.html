Why I Chose This School

Pretoria Central High School has been a home to me for my high life and it has been nothing but a pleasure to see and be a part of the school's growth within these past years.It is there where my love for the sciences and technology was developed and groomed thus leading me to the current field in which I am studying namely IT.


As a product of the school I keep reflecting on the memories of my experience at this school that made my high school life what I would describe as awesome and filled with love and how this was made possible by the staff, the school environment and extra-curricular activities done at the school among many other things.


Thus this website allows one either be a student of the school, parent or anyone interested to get a glimpse of what this great school has to offer and is all about; hence my first thought when given this task was about how it would be an honor for me to apply what I love being IT to be of service to my beloved high school Pretoria Central High School.
